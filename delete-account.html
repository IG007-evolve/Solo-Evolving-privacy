How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Solo Evolving account and all associated data directly from within the app.
  1. Open the Solo Evolving app.
  2. Go to the Settings screen (tap the gear icon in the bottom tab bar).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice in the dialog.
What happens next?
Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.
Can't delete your account from the app?
If you are unable to delete your account using the steps above, please email us at support@yourdomain.com with your account details and deletion request.
